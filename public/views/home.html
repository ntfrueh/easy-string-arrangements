If you are a string teacher in search of quality but easy-to-play string arrangements for your beginning or intermediate string ensemble, then you’ve come to the right place!

What We Offer And How We Work
First, we try to keep things simple and affordable. What we DON’T offer are hardcopies of our arrangements along with the printing and shipping costs that we have to pass onto you. What we DO offer are full scores and individual parts as PDF files and an MP3 file of the arrangement - all of which are emailed to you once you make a purchase.

The Instrumentation
In addition to offering traditional violin, viola, cello and string bass parts, we also offer a piano part. Some arrangements include the chord changes within the piano part while other arrangements include a separate lead sheet. This opens the possibilities for you to include students who don’t play violin, viola, cello or bass, but do play chordal instruments like guitar or ukulele. And unlike most string arrangements, we also include a drum set or percussion part with our arrangements. The drum set notation is based upon the Percussive Arts Society (PAS) format.

What You’ll See On This Website
Sample violin and partial scores are available for all arrangements. Full MP3 files are also available so you can hear the entire piece.
